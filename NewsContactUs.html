News



Unveils 2023 Film Production Report



The Centre National du Cinéma et de l'Image Animée (CNC) has released its annual report on film production in France. The data indicates a steady growth in the number of films produced, with a notable increase in international co-productions. This trend underscores France's commitment to fostering global cinematic collaborations.

SODEC Supports 14 New Feature Films



The Société de développement des entreprises culturelles (SODEC) has announced funding for 14 new feature-length fiction films. This initiative aims to bolster the Quebec film industry, encouraging diverse storytelling and supporting local talent in bringing their visions to the big screen.

OIF is Back with 30 Films and Series Projects



The Organisation Internationale de la Francophonie (OIF) has committed to supporting 30 film and television projects, including 10 South-South co-productions. This move highlights the OIF's dedication to promoting cultural diversity and strengthening the global Francophone audiovisual industry.
