France Coproduction facilitates international cooperation with French audiovisual production companies. It allows international partners to easily identify competent French production companies ready for co-productions. The association provides detailed and up-to-date information on its members, including their skills, their previous co-productions, and their ability to engage in new projects.

For French companies, France Coproduction offers access to data on country-specific coproduction rules, key contacts, as well as resources available abroad, such as fixers, translators, and technicians. In summary, France Coproduction creates a bridge between French producers and the global market, by providing the necessary tools to facilitate international co-productions and the connection of technical partners.
